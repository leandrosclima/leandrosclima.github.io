About me

I am Leandro Lima, a Brazilian passionate about playing the drums. Every beat is an expression of my soul, and that's how I share my passion for music. Additionally, I love spending time with friends and family because human connection is an essential part of my life. My friendliness is a trait I value because it allows me to create genuine bonds and make every encounter fun and enjoyable.

Music, to me, is a universal language that transcends cultural and emotional barriers. Playing the drums allows me to express emotions uniquely and connect with the world in a special way. When I'm with friends and family, I truly feel at home, sharing laughter and strengthening bonds that will last a lifetime. My friendliness is just an extension of my love for life and people, making each experience a warm and enriching encounter.
